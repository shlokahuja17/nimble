⚡ Nimble

2-Player Racing Game

How to Play

2-Player Mode: Player 1 uses A and D, Player 2 uses ← → arrow keys

Challenge Mode: Click Switch Role during the game to control both players with a single keyboard. The highlighted player is the one you're currently controlling. Switch between them to keep both alive!

Avoid the red blocks and catch the gold squares for bonus points. Whoever has the most coins when the game ends wins!
